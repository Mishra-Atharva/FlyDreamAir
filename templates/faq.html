Frequently Asked Questions

FlyDreamAir Loyalty Program is a rewards program that allows you to unlock various perks and promotions on your travel when you spend certain amount on partenered companies.

Members enjoy priority boarding, seat upgrades, access to exclusive lounges, and the ability to earn miles for future travel rewards.

You can sign up by clicking on the Sign up here or clicking "SIGN UP" on the top of the page. Joining is free!

You can earn points by booking flights with FlyDreamAir and/ or using services of our partnered companies.

You can use your points to purchase flight tickets on a discounted point, upgrade seat plan, get discounta on certain hotels and more.

If you're experiencing difficulties redeeming points, it may be due to having restrictions on the selected flight, or technical issues. You can review the terms and conditions of our frequent flyer program or reach out to customer support for assistance.
